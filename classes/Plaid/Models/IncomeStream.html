Class Plaid::Models::IncomeStream < Plaid::Models::BaseModel

  • lib/plaid/models.rb

A representation of Income Stream data.

Attributes
[R] confidence

The Numeric representation of our confidence in the income data associated with this particular income stream, with 0 being the lowest confidence and 1 being the highest.
[R] days

The Numeric extent of data found for this income stream.
[R] monthly_income

The Numeric monthly income associated with the income stream.
[R] name

The String name of the entity associated with this income stream.
